Winter Color Season

People in the Winter season have cool undertones, dark hair, and bright, clear eyes. Their features create
strong contrast, making them look best in bold, icy, and vibrant colors.

Best colors: Black, white, navy, cobalt, emerald, true red, fuchsia, and icy tones.

Avoid: Warm, muted shades like beige, orange, and mustard.

Jewelry: Silver, platinum, white gold, and cool-toned gemstones (sapphire, diamond).

Style tip: Embrace contrast and clean, sharp lines for a polished, modern look.

Spring Color Season

People in the Spring season have warm or neutral-warm skin tones, golden or light red-toned hair, and clear,
bright eyes like blue, green, or light hazel. Their overall look is fresh, warm, and radiant.

Best colors: Light and warm tones like peach, coral, salmon, ivory, mint, aqua, light yellow, and warm beige.

Avoid: Cool, dark, or muted tones like gray, burgundy, and black — they dull Spring’s glow.

Jewelry: Gold, rose gold, and warm-toned stones.

Style tip: Choose light, vibrant colors and soft textures to highlight your natural warmth.

Autumn Color Season

People in the Autumn season have warm skin tones, rich golden or copper-toned hair, and deep eyes like hazel, green,
or warm brown. Their look is earthy, rich, and harmonious.

Best colors: Warm, deep, and muted shades like olive, mustard, terracotta, camel, rust, forest green, and warm browns.

Avoid: Cool or icy tones like gray, black, or blue-based pastels — they wash out Autumn’s richness.

Jewelry: Gold, bronze, and earthy-toned gemstones.

Style tip: Embrace textured fabrics and warm, grounded hues that reflect nature’s autumn palette.

Summer Color Season

People in the Summer season have cool or neutral-cool skin tones, soft ash or muted hair colors, and light,
calm eyes like gray, blue, or soft green. Their overall look is soft and low-contrast, with a refined quality.

Best colors: Soft pastels, dusty rose, lavender, powder blue, cool gray, and muted berry tones.

Avoid: Harsh or warm tones like black, orange, and mustard — they overpower Summer’s softness.

Jewelry: Silver, white gold, and delicate cool-toned stones.

Style tip: Flowing fabrics and blended, soft tones bring out your natural harmony.
